What happens to your stolen data when you refuse to pay the ransom?

The answer changed in 2025.

Platforms like Leak Bazaar now convert raw exfiltrated data into structured, sellable intelligence products. Your breach doesn't end when you refuse to pay. It begins a new lifecycle.

Four-stage processing pipeline: automated filtering, database reverse engineering, categorization into buyer segments, and manual validation.

Refusal to pay no longer eliminates the value of stolen data. Instead, it gets refined, segmented, and resold — potentially multiple times over extended periods.

How does this change your post-breach monitoring strategy?
