SnowTeam had a problem.

Ransom payment rates crashed to 28% in 2025, but they were sitting on terabytes of exfiltrated corporate data from non-paying victims.

The solution? Leak Bazaar — a platform designed to convert stolen data into structured, sellable intelligence products.

DarkWebIQ first identified the platform through direct engagement with SnowTeam, an initial access broker with ties to Akira ransomware.

When SnowTeam tried advertising on Exploit forum in March 2026, they got banned. ReHub also removed their listings. The pattern of removals concerned them.

So they migrated to T1erOne, where multiple RaaS groups now operate. The ecosystem adapts.

What started as an Akira-specific tool evolved into multi-supplier infrastructure with a 10% referral program.
