→ RESTRICT MANAGEMENT INTERFACES
Limit management interfaces to trusted IP ranges, reducing externally reachable attack surface for scanning tools like Masscan.

→ IMPLEMENT GEOLOCATION FILTERING
Block authentication attempts from regions inconsistent with your user base, disrupting geo-isolated proxy pools used by distributed systems.
