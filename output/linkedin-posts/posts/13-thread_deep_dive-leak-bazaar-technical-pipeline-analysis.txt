Leak Bazaar isn't just a data dump site. It's a four-stage processing pipeline that turns raw stolen files into structured intelligence products.

Here's how the technical architecture works:

→ STAGE 1: AUTOMATED FILTERING
ML-assisted removal of system debris (OS backups, DLLs, ISOs) plus NLP-based text analysis of document arrays. Eliminates noise, focuses on valuable content.

→ STAGE 2: DBMS REVERSE ENGINEERING
Server-side parsing of raw database dumps from SQL, SAP, Oracle exports into structured Excel/CSV outputs. Financial transactions, payroll records, counterparty data — all reformatted for buyers.

→ STAGE 3: CATEGORIZATION
Processed data segmented into buyer-oriented categories: quarterly financial reports, M&A intelligence, R&D materials including source code, personal data.

→ STAGE 4: MANUAL VALIDATION
In-house analyst review before listing. Quality control to ensure data authenticity and commercial value.

Revenue model: 70% to data supplier, 30% to platform. Exclusive purchases (full price, data removed) or shared purchases (discounted, remains available).

This transforms exfiltration from binary extortion to structured asset monetization. Multiple revenue streams from single breach.
