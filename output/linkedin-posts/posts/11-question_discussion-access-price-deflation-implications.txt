Why did network access prices crash 69% in three years?

From $1,427 in Q1 2023 to $439 in Q1 2026, according to DarkWebIQ operational data.

The answer reveals something critical about the threat landscape.

Automation, AI-assisted tooling, and oversupply from infostealer logs drove commoditization of basic network access. More actors can afford market entry, driving volume up and prices down.

But premium access still commands thousands: domain admin credentials, full Citrix environments, cloud management consoles.
