Leak Bazaar isn't just another data dump site. It's a four-stage processing pipeline designed to convert raw exfiltrated data into structured, sellable intelligence products.

→ STAGE 1: AUTOMATED FILTERING
ML-assisted removal of system debris (OS backups, DLLs, ISOs) combined with NLP-based text analysis of document arrays. The goal: eliminate noise, surface value.

→ STAGE 2: DBMS REVERSE ENGINEERING
Server-side parsing of raw database dumps from SQL, SAP, Oracle exports into structured Excel/CSV outputs. Financial transactions, payroll records, counterparty data — all formatted for buyers.

→ STAGE 3: CATEGORIZATION
Processed data segmented into buyer-oriented categories: quarterly financial reports, M&A data, R&D material, source code, personal data. No more browsing through raw dumps.

→ STAGE 4: MANUAL VALIDATION
In-house analyst review before listing. Quality control that generates structured analytical reports identifying OFAC violations, shadow accounting, unpublished financial results.

Two purchasing models: exclusive (full price, data removed after sale) or shared (lower price, data remains available for resale). Revenue split: 70% to supplier, 30% to platform.

This represents industrialization of data monetization. Organizations refusing ransoms can no longer assume their data becomes worthless.
